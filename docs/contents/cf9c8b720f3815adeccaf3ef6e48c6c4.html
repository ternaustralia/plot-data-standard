Plot-X - Plot Data Exchange Standard

Description

Plot-X is a national Australian ecological data exchange standard for exchanging plot-based survey data.

This standard is funded by the Department of Agriculture, Water and the Environment, under the Digital Environmental Assessment Program (DEAP) and the National Landcare Program. See the project poster for more information.
