PlantPopulation

UMLClass
Plot-X - Plot Data Exchange Standard :: Plot-X Vegetation :: «VegetationFeature»PlantPopulation

Description

  1. A group of individuals of the same plant species living within a given area.

  2. A group of individuals of a species with the potential to interbreed in a defined geographic region.

Source: https://people.wou.edu/~guralnl/gural/454Plant%20Population%20EcologyI.pdf
